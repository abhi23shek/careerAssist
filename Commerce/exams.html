Entrance Exams After 12th Commerce

List of Entrance Exams After 12th for Commerce Students

Here’s a list of some popular entrance exams after 12th for commerce students in India. These exams open doors to various specialized programs and career paths in the field.

Organization Entrance Exam Objective
Institute of Chartered Accountants in India (ICAI) CA Foundation Entry-level exam for aspiring Chartered Accountants.
Institute of Company Secretary in India (ICSI) Company Secretary Executive Entrance Exam (CSEET) Admission test for students pursuing the Company Secretaryship Course.
Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI) CMA Entrance Exam Conducted by ICAI for courses at three levels: foundation, intermediate, and final.
Institute of Actuaries of India (IAI) ACET Exam for individuals aspiring to become actuaries.
CFA Institute CFA Designation for those completing the CFA® program and work experience requirements.
American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) CPA Equivalent to Chartered Accountant (CA) designation in India, conducted by AICPA.
St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai St Xavier Entrance Test Qualifies students for admission to BMS and BMM programs at St. Xavier’s College.
Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) NMIMS – NPAT Conducted for admission to undergraduate programs like BBA and B.Com.
National Testing Agency (NTA) Common University Entrance Test (CUET) For undergraduate admissions at 44 central universities and 46 other institutions.
Symbiosis International University SET Qualifies candidates for admission to various UG courses.
Indian Institute of Management Indore IPMAT National-level test for admission to the five-year Integrated Program in Management (IPM).
Patna Women’s College PUCET (Patna Women’s College B.Com Entrance Exam) Admission tests for undergraduate courses like BA (Hons) and B.Com (Hons) at Patna Women’s College.
Lovely Professional University LPU-NEST National entrance and scholarship test (LPUNEST) for admission in 150+ programs at Lovely Professional University.
