Top Commerce Colleges in India

Top Commerce Colleges

Here is a list of top commerce colleges in India known for their academic excellence and strong placement records:

  • Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), Delhi: Established in 1926, SRCC is recognized for its commerce education. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate education, and organizations like Deloitte, KPMG, and Ernst & Young actively recruit from here.
  • Lady Shri Ram College for Women (LSR), Delhi: LSR, affiliated with the University of Delhi, provides rigorous education in commerce and economics. Graduates often find placements in companies like PwC, EY, and KPMG.
  • Loyola College, Chennai: A prestigious autonomous college, Loyola offers various commerce courses and maintains strong industry connections. Companies like Goldman Sachs and Amazon hire graduates from here.
  • St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai: Established in 1869, St. Xavier’s College is noted for its academic quality and provides a wide range of courses in arts, science, commerce, and management. EY, KPMG, and Deloitte are some top recruiters.
  • Christ University, Bangalore: Christ University, a private institution, offers diverse commerce and management courses. It’s recognized for academic prowess and industry interactions, attracting recruiters like Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley.
  • Hindu College, Delhi: Affiliated with the University of Delhi, Hindu College is highly regarded for its commerce and economics programs. Graduates secure placements in companies such as JSW, KPMG, and EY.
  • Hansraj College, Delhi: Hansraj College offers courses in arts, commerce, economics, and science. Graduates find opportunities in sectors like consulting, finance, and FMCG, with recruiters including Bain & Co. and Deloitte.
  • Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai: With A+ accreditation, this college offers a range of courses. It has a robust industry interface, attracting companies like Deloitte, EY, and Google.
  • St. Joseph’s College of Commerce, Bangalore: Recognized as A++ Grade by NAAC, this college offers courses in commerce, management, humanities, and more. Graduates are sought after by companies like Amazon and EY.
  • Madras Christian College (MCC), Chennai: MCC offers diverse courses in commerce, economics, social work, and more. Graduates secure positions in companies such as Deloitte and KPMG due to the college’s strong academic foundation and infrastructure.
