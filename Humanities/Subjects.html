Humanities Subjects

Mandatory and Optional Subjects

In Class 11, students pursuing the Humanities stream are required to study a set of mandatory subjects and choose from optional subjects. Here's a breakdown:

  • Mandatory Subjects: These are required for all Humanities students.
  • 5th Optional Subject (Imperative to Choose): You must select one primary optional subject according to your interest.
  • 6th Optional Subject (Not Imperative to Choose): You may select a secondary optional subject from the provided list.

Take a look at the table below to know the mandatory and optional subjects for the Humanities stream in Class 11 CBSE:
