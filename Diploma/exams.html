Diploma Courses Entrance Exams

Entrance Exam Description
Chhattisgarh Pre Polytechnic Test (CG PPT) An entrance exam conducted by the Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CGPEB) for admission into diploma courses in engineering and polytechnic institutions in Chhattisgarh.
Central Institute of Plastics Engineering and Technology (CIPET) CIPET JEE Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) conducted by CIPET for admission into various diploma and degree programs related to plastics engineering and technology at CIPET campuses.
Delhi CET Delhi Common Entrance Test (CET) is conducted by the Department of Training and Technical Education (DTTE) for admission to various diploma courses in engineering and technology across Delhi.
Assam Polytechnic Admission Exam (Assam PAT) An entrance exam conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) Assam for admission into diploma courses in polytechnic institutions in Assam.
BTER JEEP Joint Entrance Exam for Polytechnic (JEECUP) conducted by the Board of Technical Education Rajasthan (BTER) for admission into various diploma programs in polytechnic institutions in Rajasthan.
CITD Entrance Exam Entrance test conducted by the Central Institute of Tool Design (CITD) for admission into diploma programs in tool design and engineering.
Pondicherry University Entrance Exam Entrance exam conducted by Pondicherry University for admission into various diploma and degree programs offered by the university.
JEECUP Joint Entrance Examination Council Uttar Pradesh (JEECUP) for admission into various polytechnic diploma courses in Uttar Pradesh.
VOCLET Vocational Lateral Entry Test (VOCLET) conducted by the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education for admission into second-year diploma programs in polytechnic institutions.
GKCIET Entrance Exam Entrance test conducted by the Gobardanga Kshudiram College of Engineering and Technology (GKCIET) for admission into various diploma courses offered by the institute.
