Medical - Possible Careers

MBBS

The MBBS program lasts for five years followed by a 1-year internship. The MBBS program is an undergraduate program designed for individuals who aspire to become doctors. A professional degree in medical science is called MBBS.

Full Form of MBBS

The full form of MBBS is Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery.

Candidates must be over 17 years old and have completed Class 12 in the Science stream with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) courses to be eligible to enroll in the MBBS program. The cost of an MBBS program typically ranges from INR 25,000 to INR 1.15 crore. The only eligibility criterion for MBBS admission is NEET. Upon completion of the MBBS program, applicants will be eligible to practice medicine or become doctors. An MBBS graduate typically makes between INR 2.5 and 15 lakhs per annum.

BAMS

The study of Ayurvedic medicine is the main subject of the BAMS professional medical course. Students who successfully complete five and a half years of study, including a year-long internship, are awarded a degree. In some Indian states, a BAMS graduate may practice medicine after obtaining a license from an organization that has been approved by the government. A detailed understanding of “Ashtanga Ayurveda” is taught in the BAMS program, together with cutting-edge scientific discoveries in contemporary medicine and a great deal of hands-on training.

Full Form of BAMS

The full form of BAMS is Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery.

Candidates must have passed Class 12 in the Science stream with 50% to 60% in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) subjects in order to be eligible to enroll in the BAMS course. To get admitted to BAMS, candidates must have also passed NEET. For BAMS courses, only the NEET scores are allowed. The annual cost of the BAMS course fees is between INR 20,000 and INR 3,00,000.

Following graduation from a BAMS program, applicants may choose to pursue careers as Ayurvedic doctors, category managers, business development officers, or other positions like assistant professors or associate professors in colleges. A graduate of a BAMS program typically makes between INR 3 LPA and INR 15 LPA.

GNM

GNM is a three-year program followed by a six-month internship. Students learn about maternity care, post-trauma rehabilitation, mental health, data collection, and other nursing practices in this course.

Full Form of GNM

The full form of GNM is General Nursing Midwifery.

In order to be admitted to the GNM program, students must receive at least 50% in their class 12 exams. A GNM training typically costs between INR 40,000 and INR 1 lakh. An annual remuneration of INR 2 lakh to INR 5 lakh is typical for a graduate in GNM.

BDS

BDS is a five-year undergraduate program with four years dedicated to theory and one year to a rotating internship. Students must pass the required qualification exam, which is administered once a year across the country, in order to be admitted to a BDS program. Candidates for BDS prepare for professions in dentistry surgery. In order to be eligible for this course, interested individuals must pass the NEET exam.

Full Form of BDS

The full form of BDS is Bachelor of Dental Surgery.

A BDS program typically costs between INR 1 lakh and INR 6 lakh annually in course fees. An annual income of INR 4 lakh to INR 10 lakh is typical for a BDS graduate.

BHMS

BHMS uses liquid or pill forms of very diluted homeopathic medications to treat patients to support their body’s natural healing process. In India, the BHMS undergraduate degree in homeopathy is conferred following a five-and-a-half-year study period that includes a one-year internship. Candidates who choose to pursue BHMS as their medical program can learn about homeopathic medicine in addition to receiving treatment. In order to treat patients with homeopathic expertise, candidates can choose from a variety of specializations, including pediatrics, psychiatry, skincare, and homeopathic pharmacy.

Full Form of BHMS

The full form of BHMS is Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery.

Students must have completed class 12 with a minimum of 50% of the required points in order to be eligible for the BHMS course. In India, the typical course cost for BHMS programs ranges from INR 12k to INR 2 lakh. An annual salary of INR 8 lakh is typical for BHMS graduates.

ANM

ANM is a two-year diploma program. ANM teaches students how to set up operating rooms, maintain equipment, keep records, and give patients their medications on time.

Full Form of ANM

The full form of ANM is Auxiliary Nursing Midwifery.

In order to be admitted to the ANM program, students must receive a minimum of 50% overall in their class 12 exams. An annual cost of INR 1.5 lakh to 2 lakh is typical for the ANM program. Graduates in ANM often make between INR 3 lakh and INR 5 lakh annually.
