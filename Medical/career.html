Medical - Possible Careers

MBBS

The MBBS program is designed for individuals aspiring to become doctors. It includes:

  • Duration: 5 years + 1-year internship
  • Eligibility: Age 17+, Class 12 with PCB
  • Entrance Exam: NEET
  • Cost: INR 25,000 to INR 1.15 crore
  • Annual Salary: INR 2.5 to 15 lakhs

BAMS

The BAMS course focuses on Ayurvedic medicine. Key details include:

  • Duration: 5.5 years + 1-year internship
  • Eligibility: Class 12 with 50-60% in PCB
  • Entrance Exam: NEET
  • Cost: INR 20,000 to INR 3,00,000
  • Annual Salary: INR 3 LPA to INR 15 LPA
