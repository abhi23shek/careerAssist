Allen Career Institute

Initially, it began with eight students and later expanded to cover subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

With a dedicated team and visionary leadership, Allen has consistently achieved remarkable success, including producing All India Ranks 1,2,3 in NEET from its classroom programs. Today, it’s a trusted institution with over 28 lakh students and an excellent reputation.
