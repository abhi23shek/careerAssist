Medical - Subjects

Subjects in 11th Class

Physics

Physics includes the study of matter, energy, motion, and force. It focuses on comprehending the underlying ideas that guide the universe. You will study how things in the universe work. When you choose to study the medical stream, the subject of physics allows you to study various topics such as motion, units and measurements, friction, center of mass, moment of inertia, elastic behavior of solids, thermal equilibrium and temperature, and heat, work, and internal energy.

Chemistry

In chemistry, you will study the characteristics and making of reactions, known as chemistry. It investigates the materials that make up everything in our environment. Chemistry might be for you if you enjoy running experiments and learning about the inner workings of chemical reactions. Studying chemistry opens many future opportunities, including becoming a chemist after completing higher education.

English

All students must take English, regardless of their stream. English is widely spoken worldwide, and the subject covers various topics, including comprehension, unseen passages, and grammar. Studying English is essential to securing good marks.

Biology

Biology is the study of how living things interact with each other and their surroundings. It includes topics related to individual cells and entire ecosystems. Biology may be the best career choice if you're interested in healthcare or conservation and are captivated by life in all its forms.
