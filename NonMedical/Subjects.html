PCM Subjects for Class 11th & 12th

PCM Subjects Class 11th

In Class 11, students have the option to choose between different streams, and PCM is one of the most popular choices among science students. Along with PCM subjects, students usually study a language subject and an elective subject. The PCM subjects for Class 11 are as follows:

  • Physics: Physics deals with the study of matter, energy, motion, and forces. Topics covered include mechanics, heat, thermodynamics, optics, and more.
  • Chemistry: Chemistry focuses on the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of substances. Students learn about elements, compounds, chemical reactions, and various branches of chemistry.
  • Mathematics: Mathematics involves the study of numbers, quantity, space, and patterns. Topics include algebra, trigonometry, calculus, coordinate geometry, and statistics.
  • English: English is a language subject that emphasizes communication, comprehension, and language skills.
  • Elective Subject: The elective subject could vary depending on the school or board of education. Common elective subjects in the PCM stream may include Computer Science, Biology, Economics, Physical Education, or other subjects.

PCM Subjects Class 12th

In Class 12, students continue with the PCM stream, which remains a popular choice for those interested in pursuing careers in science, engineering, and related fields. Along with the core PCM subjects, students usually study a language subject and an elective subject. The general subjects for Class 12 are as follows:

  • Physics: In Class 12 Physics, students delve deeper into topics such as electromagnetism, optics, modern physics, and more complex mechanics. This builds upon the foundation laid in Class 11 and prepares them for advanced studies in physics-related disciplines.
  • Chemistry: Class 12 Chemistry further explores areas like organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, and physical chemistry. Students gain a comprehensive understanding of chemical reactions and the properties of different elements and compounds.
  • Mathematics: Mathematics in Class 12 covers advanced topics in calculus, algebra, trigonometry, and analytical geometry. This subject is crucial for students pursuing engineering and other math-intensive fields.
  • English: English continues to be a language subject emphasizing communication and language skills.
  • Elective Subject: The elective subject in Class 12 can vary depending on the school or board of education. Students can choose from a range of subjects such as Computer Science, Biology, Economics, Physical Education, or other subjects.
