Popular Entrance Exams for Non-Medical in India

  1. JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination Main)

    This is a national-level examination organized by the National Testing Agency (NTA) in India. It's conducted for students who want to apply for B.E./B.Tech courses in the top engineering colleges in India, including the NITs, IITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions (CFTIs).

  2. JEE Advanced

    The JEE Advanced is the second stage of the IIT Joint Entrance Examination. Only the top-ranking students from JEE Main are allowed to appear for this exam, which is for admission to undergraduate courses at the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and a few other top engineering institutes.

  3. SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination)

    This is a university-level entrance test conducted by the SRM Institute of Science and Technology for admission to its B.Tech programs. It offers a wide range of specializations in engineering, and the entrance test is taken by a large number of students across India.

  4. WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination)

    This is a state-level entrance test conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board. The exam serves as a gateway for admissions into B.E., B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Pharma courses in various colleges and universities across West Bengal.

  5. KIITEE

    The Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology Entrance Examination (KIITEE) is an entrance exam conducted by KIIT University. It's a university-level exam for students who wish to gain admission to a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses offered by the university. These include engineering, law, computer applications, nursing, and many more.

  6. CUSAT CAT

    The Cochin University of Science and Technology Common Admission Test (CUSAT CAT) is conducted by Cochin University. This exam is a gateway for students looking to secure a place in undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the fields of engineering and science. CUSAT CAT is a university-level exam and the admissions are open to international students as well.

  7. NATA

    The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the Council of Architecture (COA). This exam is for students who aspire to pursue undergraduate courses in Architecture. It evaluates students' aptitude for the specific field of study by assessing their drawing and observation skills, sense of proportion, aesthetic sensitivity, and critical thinking ability.

  8. HP CET

    The Himachal Pradesh Common Entrance Test (HP CET) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU). This exam is conducted for admission into various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fields such as engineering, pharmacy, management, and computer applications in the colleges of Himachal Pradesh.

  9. IPU CET (Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test)

    This is a university-level entrance exam conducted by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). It's designed for students seeking admission into various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the fields of engineering, medical, dental, law, and management.
